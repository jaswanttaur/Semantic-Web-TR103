Rose

Roses are one of the most popular flowers in the world. They are known for their beauty, fragrance, and symbolism of love and passion. Roses come in various colors and each color has its own significance. Red roses symbolize love, white roses signify purity, and yellow roses represent friendship.

Tulip

Tulips are vibrant and colorful flowers that bloom in the spring. They are associated with renewal and the coming of warmer weather. Tulips are available in a wide range of colors including red, yellow, pink, and purple. They are often seen as a symbol of perfect love and are a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
