RDF: Resource Description Framework, a standard for data interchange on the web.

NFR (Non-Functional Requirement): Criteria that judge the operation of a system, such as security, performance, and reliability.

Functional requirements: Specific features or functions of a system (e.g., RAM i3, software, server). Focus on these to meet user needs.
Web3 says focus on non-functional requirements:
- Security: Protecting data and resources from unauthorized access.
- Performance: How fast the system responds and processes requests.
- Reliability: Consistency in the system's performance and availability.
